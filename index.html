We Make getting from A to B fun.

So how does this actually work? Put an engineer, an architect, and two computer programmers who also happen to all ride bikes together and have a passion for community greatness. The computery brains behind Rickshaw Photobooth is a Raspberry Pi micro computer. Upon request, this computer will capture, process and output a series of images. Photos are instantly processed and uploaded to an online gallery and shared over social media. At the end of the ride users have the option to scan a QR code to download images to their mobile phones.
